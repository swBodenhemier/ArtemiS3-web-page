ArtemiS3: an intelligent search for AWS S3 buckets

Data is at the heart of discovery. From planetary missions to global research, organizations like NASA and USGS rely on data stored in the cloud. However accessing and making sense of this data is complex and time consuming. ArtemiS3 addresses this challenge by developing an intelligent platform to index, search, and visualize publicly accessible AWS S3 buckets used by NASA's Planetary Data System.
